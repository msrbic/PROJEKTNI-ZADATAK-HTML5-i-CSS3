The lion king

Disney has just dropped a new trailer for The Lion King live action remake set to be released in 2019. The trailer was first shown during the Washington at Dallas football game on FOX.

The Lion King is a CGI hybrid style of film similar to The Jungle Book, which was also directed by Jon Favreau. The film stars the voices of Donald Glover, Beyoncé, James Earl Jones, Chiwetel Ejiofor, Billy Eichner, Seth Rogen, John Kani, John Oliver, and Alfre Woodard, all of which are announced in this trailer.

For every update about this new film, slated to be released on July 19th, 2019, stay updated here on WDWNT and follow us on social media.
